Smart Fridge: Reducing Food Waste with AI

About the Project

Food waste is a critical issue that impacts both the environment and the economy on a global scale. Our Smart Fridge project leverages advanced machine learning techniques to monitor and quantify the spoilage of fruits and vegetables in real time, helping reduce household food waste.
